Genussregion Zurich

A culinary festival platform spanning ten months across the Zurich region — built in 10 days with a 4-person team and 160 commits.

Team Unchained Commerce
Role Core Contributor
Live Site genussregion-zuerich.ch

The Challenge

Genussregion Zurich 2026 needed a comprehensive platform to showcase events, partners, news, and brand guidelines for a culinary festival featuring hundreds of hosts, producers, and food startups. The critical constraint: the entire platform had to be built and launched within 10 days.

The Solution

We implemented a modern headless architecture using Next.js 14 with React Server Components, GraphQL for data querying, Cockpit CMS for content management, and Unchained Engine 4.5 on the backend. The architecture enabled parallel feature development across the 4-person team and future scalability.

Key Features

Event System

Event browsing with filtering and registration, supporting hundreds of events across the ten-month festival period.

Dynamic CMS

20+ dynamic content block types managed via Cockpit CMS, enabling rich editorial pages and flexible layouts.

Regional News

News system with tag-based navigation and regional filtering, keeping visitors informed about festival happenings.

Search & Design System

Full-text search across pages, news, and events. Documented style guide for consistent brand identity and future development.
