Role Core Contributor

The Challenge

Lorem GmbH needed a modern B2B platform that could manage an extensive product catalog of 20,000+ items, implement customer-specific pricing and discount structures, integrate seamlessly with their Selectline ERP, and digitize their quote processes — all while preserving established business workflows.

The Solution

We built a headless e-commerce architecture decoupling frontend and backend systems. The platform features intuitive content management via Cockpit CMS, mobile-first responsive design, and a flexible pricing engine supporting tiered discounts and campaigns.

Key Features

Content Management

Drag-and-drop interface for updates, real-time preview, multilingual support, asset management, and role-based access controls.

Customer Experience

Personalized dashboards with order history, dynamic pricing based on agreements, one-click reordering for frequent purchases, advanced search with product recommendations, and installed base tracking.

ERP Integration

Real-time inventory synchronization with Selectline, automated order processing, cross-system customer data sync, and a digitized quotation process replacing manual workflows.

Search & Discovery

Solr-powered full-text search across 20,000+ articles with faceted filtering, product recommendations, and fast results delivery.
